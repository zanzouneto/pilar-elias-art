Pilar Elias is an accomplished artist hailing from Lebanon, where the vibrant hues of her acrylic paintings reflect her unique artistic vision. Her work is an exploration of colors and emotions.
Pilar's journey into the world of art began later in life, but it was a love affair that sparked her creative spirit and set her on a path of unwavering dedication.
